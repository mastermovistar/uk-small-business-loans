Understanding Your Financing Options

There are several financing options available to London start-ups, including cash flow finance, equipment leasing and hire purchase, and merchant cash advances. Each option has its pros and cons, and it's essential to understand which one is best suited for your business. For instance, if you're looking to purchase new equipment, equipment leasing and hire purchase might be the way to go. On the other hand, if you need quick access to cash, merchant cash advances could be the ideal solution.

Secured vs Unsecured Business Loans

When it comes to business loans, you have the option to choose between secured business loans and unsecured business loans. Secured loans require collateral, which can be a significant advantage if you have assets to offer. However, unsecured loans are often preferred by start-ups as they don't require collateral and offer more flexibility. Our team at Asra Financial can help you determine which option is best for your business.

As a London-based business, you may be eligible for location-specific financing options. For example, if you're based in Camden or Richmond, you may have access to local funding initiatives.
